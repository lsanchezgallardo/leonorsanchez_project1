Top 5 Favorite Straw Hat Pirate Crew Members

Home My Inspiration Top 5's My Portfolio

  1. Monkey D. Luffy

    Monkey D. Luffy is the main character and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is my favorite character because he is a loyal and determined guy with a goofy personality. He has a big heart and is always willing to help his friends when danger is near. He is strong and lives freely, which is admirable. He ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi (the real name of it being the Hito Hito no Mi, model: Nika (Sun God/Warrior of Liberation) - the Human Human fruit), which gives him the ability to stretch his body like rubber.

  2. Roronoa Zoro

    Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the first mate to join the crew and he is the right hand man of the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a skilled fighter and a loyal friend. He has a strong sense of justice and is always ready to protect his crew.

  3. Tony Tony Chopper

    Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a reindeer who ate the Hito Hito no Mi, which gives him the ability to transform into a human-reindeer hybrid. He is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates and is always willing to help his crew and others when they are injured. He is also a cotton candy lover.

  4. Vinsmoke Sanji

    Vinsmoke Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a skilled chef and a powerful fighter, making him the left hand man of the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He never uses his hands to fight, only his legs, because he believes that a cook should never get his hands dirty. He is known for his love for women, which is a big part of his personality. He is a loyal friend and is always willing to help his crew when they are in trouble.

  5. "Soul King" Brook

    "Soul King" Brook is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a living skeleton who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, which gives him the ability to come back to life after dying. He is a skilled musician and a powerful swordsman, having the ability to use his soul and ice to fight. He is also known for his love for music and singing.

Top 5 Favorite Supporting Characters of One Piece


  • Portgas D. Ace

    Portgas D. Ace is the second division commander of the Whitebeard pirates and also one of Luffy's sworn older brothers. He is my favorite supporting character because is a strong and caring guy, who is always looking out for Luffy. He is son of the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger and he ate the Mera Mera no Mi, which gives him the ability to create and control fire.

  • Sabo

    Sabo is the chief of staff of the Revolutionary Army (led by Monkey D. Dragon, Luffy's father) and is also one of Luffy's sworn older brothers. He is a strong and caring guy, who is always looking out for Luffy. He is the second son of the noble family of the Goa Kingdom and he ate the Mera Mera no Mi (which was Ace's fruit before he died), which gives him the ability to create and control fire.

  • Trafalgar D. Water Law

    Trafalgar D. Water Law is the captain of the Heart Pirates and is a skilled doctor. He is a strategic and intelligent guy, who is always looking out for his crew. He ate the Ope Ope no Mi, which gives him the ability to create a spherical territory called "Room" where he can manipulate anything, including himself and others.

  • Edward Newgate (Whitebeard)

    Edward Newgate, also known as Whitebeard, was the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and was one of the Four Emperors of the New World. He was a powerful and caring man, who was always looking out for his crew, which he called his family. He ate the Gura Gura no Mi, which gives him the ability to create shockwaves and cause earthquakes. He was widely respected and feared throughout the seas.

  • Gol D. Roger

    Gol D. Roger, also known as the Pirate King, was the captain of the Roger Pirates. He was the most powerful and influential pirate in history, who was always looking out for his crew. He was the first person to reach the end of the Grand Line and discover the One Piece, which is the greatest treasure in the world. He was executed by the World Government, but his legacy lives on. He was also the father of Portgas D. Ace.
