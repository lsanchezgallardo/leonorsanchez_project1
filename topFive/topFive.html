  1. Monkey D. Luffy


    2. Monkey D. Luffy is the main character and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is my favorite character because he is a loyal and determined guy with a goofy personality. He has a big heart and is always willing to help his friends when danger is near. He is strong and lives freely, which is admirable. He ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi (the real name of it being the Hito Hito no Mi, model: Nika (Sun God/Warrior of Liberation) - the Human Human fruit), which gives him the ability to stretch his body like rubber.
