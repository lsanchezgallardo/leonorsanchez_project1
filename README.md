# Technical Overview

### Structure

Project 3 will be a webpage built with HTML and CSS. Users could interact with my webpage by clicking on the navigation words which are visually simple to identify based on the structure of my site. Design and imagery will display on every page, with animations and motions. There are 4 pages; About, Inspiration, Top 5, and Portfolio. The About page is the home screen which includes my self-portrait, GIF, logo element, navigation bar, header with my name, text animation, and email link. The Inspiration page includes my three entries with context and external links to the websites of the entries. The Top 5 page includes 2 lists of my favorite anime characters with context, links, and images. Crediting the creator and animation production company at the bottom of the page. Lastly, the Portfolio page including images of my projects and context. Every page consists of an icon/logo element at the top right corner that I hand drew. 

Including:
