My name is Leonor Sanchez Gallardo

My name is Leonor Sanchez and I am a transfer student from Santa Rosa Junior College, attending my second semester at San Francisco State University. After a long journey of trying to discover my passion, I finalized my decision and chose to major in Visual Communication Design in hopes of taking on a freelance graphic design and entrepreneurship role in the near future. I see myself designing my own small business in the future and working for businesses that I love and support. I was always drawn to art and design as a child because of how uniquely striking it is to the eye. Seeing the unique art people create is truly inspiring and motivational. I've always been the person to admire the little details that go unacknowledged. Aside from design, there are other things I enjoy like fitness, photography, cooking, reading, fashion and music. I like to read educational books involving things I want to learn about and I enjoy cooking my favorite Carribean dishes when I get the chance. I never expected to pursue a career in this field after several trials and errors, but here I am; eager to learn and thrilled to begin my new educational journey! From me to you; follow what your heart wants, rather than what your mind thinks it wants.
