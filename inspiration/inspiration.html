Inspirational Artists

By Leonor Sanchez Gallardo

Valfré

IIse Valfré is a Mexican artist from Tijuana, with vibrant visual designs, paintings, illustrations, and merchandise. Her designs reflect on the themes of identity, beauty, culture, imagination and emotional expression. I'm inspired by her bold designs and intentional expression.

Valfré

Tatiana Cardona

Tatiana Cardona is a ceramic artist from Colombia, known for her brand Female Alchemy. Her ceramics are inspired by influential female figures who played a impactful role in her life. Her culture is reflected in her work, as well as her childhood, womanhood, and fond memories. I am inspired by her work; how she connects her art to her culture and personal journey.

Female Alchemy

Mada

Mada is an artist from New York City, known for her vibrant and unique paintings and designs inspired by her identity and exploration. Her are is heavily influenced by her interests and current trends. The fun colors and funky themes in her work inspire me to be more creative and expressive with my own work.
